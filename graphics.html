Merchandise Design

Over the years of designing I have acquired an interest in designing merchandise. I have worked on many projects, mostly for the AEPi fraternity. As the Merchandise Chair for the Lambda Deuteron chapter at the University of Miami I have worked on t-shirts for multiple occasions.
